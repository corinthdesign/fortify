Marriage Intervention

There are times when a husband and wife are perpetually wounding each other. Instead of growing, the marriage is spiraling downward towards collapse. Destructive words, unresolved conflict, acts of betrayal, and deep-seeded bitterness can have a marriage on the brink of divorce. We know because we have been there.

BUT WE ALSO KNOW THE MIRACLE OF RELATIONAL HEALING AND A RESTORED MARRIAGE.

This healing comes from the supernatural power of the Holy Spirit, the Truth of Scripture, and the commitment of a husband and wife to learn a better way of doing life together.

Fortify provides weekend to week-long interventions for married couples in crisis. We especially want pastors and their spouses to know that we are a safe place to turn when you don’t feel like you can open up with anyone in your local setting of ministry. We served as a local church pastoral couple for 25 years, so we understand the unique challenges pastors and their spouses face.

Don’t wait any longer. There is hope! Contact us today. We believe your marriage can be all that God designed it to be!
