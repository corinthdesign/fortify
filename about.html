Our Founders

Kevin and Barbara Goos, Founders of Fortify

From 1991-2016, Kevin and Barbara served in local church and regional leadership positions. After facing a crisis that could have ended their marriage and ministry, Kevin and Barbara walked through a miraculous journey of healing and restoration. They are more in love than ever and are unified together in the Lord as a couple. They have been married for 30 years, and are blessed with four children, a daughter-in-law, 2 son-in-laws, and 5 grandchildren.
