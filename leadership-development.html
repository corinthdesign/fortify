Leadership Development

Howard Hendricks used to say that every person needs a Paul, a Timothy, and a Silas in their life. He said Paul represents someone building into you who has more experience than you do. Timothy represents someone you are investing in who has less experience than you do. Silas represents and equal or peer who loves you but isn't impressed by you.

LEADERSHIP DEVELOPMENT IS A VOLUNTARY JOURNEY OF GROWTH IN WHICH YOU STRATEGICALLY INVITE KEY PEERS, AS WELL AS LEADERS WHO ARE FURTHER DOWN THE ROAD, TO HELP YOU TOWARDS YOUR GOALS OF PERSONAL DEVELOPMENT.
